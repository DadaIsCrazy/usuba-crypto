USUBA is a bitslicing, optimized and trustworthy compiler

Bitslicing

Bitslicing consists in reducing an algorithm to bitwise operations, which increases performance by exploiting dataparallelism, while improving security.

Optimized

USUBA generates optimized C code that runs fast, and makes all required optimizations.

Trustworthy

To ensure that our optimizations are always correct, and that the bitslicing always work as intended, USUBA is proven.

What can you do with USUBA?

Cryptography

Bitslicing significantly improves the performance of certain algorithmic operations. We have shown in our papers that USUBA runs AES faster than rival state-of-the-art compilers.

IOT

With the proliferation of micro-processors and other embedded electronics, security is a paramount concern. USUBA can help protect IOT electronics again several types of attacks.

Research

USUBA is a great platform to make research on bitslicing. We also encourage compilation professors to use our compiler to teach their students about bitslicing, optimization, and how to prove code.
